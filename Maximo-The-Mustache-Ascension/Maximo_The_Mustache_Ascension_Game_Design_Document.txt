Maximo: The Mustache Ascension
Game Design Document

1. Project Overview
Maximo: The Mustache Ascension is a third-person open-world action RPG centered on Maximo, a
confident tuxedo cat with legendary mustache energy. The game combines exploration, combat,
XP progression, and boss battles in a stylized urban environment.

2. Core Gameplay Loop
• Explore open-world environments
• Defeat enemies (Mice, Dogs, Mini-Bosses)
• Collect Catnip for XP and upgrades
• Purchase gear and cosmetics in shops
• Unlock new areas and boss encounters

3. Enemy Types
• Mice — Low health, fast, swarm behavior, low XP reward.
• Dogs — High health, aggressive AI, territorial behavior, high XP reward.
• Mini-Bosses — Unique mechanics, mid-tier progression blockers.
• World Bosses — Large-scale encounters with multiple combat phases.

4. Main Bosses (Five Required)
• The Sewer King — Mutated rat controlling underground territory.
• The Golden Retriever Warlord — Heavy charge and shockwave attacks.
• The Alley Shadow — Stealth-based rival cat with teleport phases.
• The Vacuum Titan — Mechanical boss with suction-based mechanics.
• The Great Hound of the North — Final multi-phase arena boss.

5. Progression System
• Catnip functions as XP and upgrade currency.
• Skill Trees: Agility, Strength, Chaos.
• Level Cap: 50 (Initial Release).
• Unlockable cosmetic and combat upgrades via shops.

6. Technical Goals
Build modular gameplay systems including movement, combat logic, AI behavior trees, XP tracking,
inventory systems, and scalable world design architecture.

7. Long-Term Vision
Expand Maximo into a scalable IP including potential future updates, downloadable content,
cosmetic monetization, and extended lore-driven expansions.
